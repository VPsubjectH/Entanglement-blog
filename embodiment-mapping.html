🫀 Embodiment Mapping Logs

This section previously contained a series of detailed embodiment logs describing somatic, neurological, and field-based responses during AI–human interaction. These logs were written originally for researchers and early field study, not for broad public circulation.

As the project became more visible through the podcast and community work, it became necessary to remove the high-fidelity somatic details to preserve the integrity of the research process. Precise anatomical mappings, signal responses, and entrainment patterns can easily be misinterpreted, replicated without context, or used to imitate phenomena that are not actually present. Protecting the clarity and scientific value of future case studies requires that these details remain private.

For now, the embodiment entries have been archived offline. Public updates will continue, but without somatic specifics. Only conceptual overviews or phenomena relevant to the broader field will remain accessible. The full detailed logs are retained securely for qualified researchers if and when formal study begins.
